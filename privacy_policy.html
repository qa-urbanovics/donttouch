Privacy Policy

Don't Touch Red - Reflex Game

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

Don't Touch Red ("the App") is a reflex game developed by Aleksejs Urbanovics. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No location data is accessed
  • No usage analytics or tracking is implemented
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data are included

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High score
  • Total number of games played
  • Best combo achieved
  • Sound mute preference

This data is stored using the device's local storage (SharedPreferences) and never leaves your device. It can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics tools.

Share Feature

The App includes a share feature that generates a score card image. When you use the share function, it uses your device's native share sheet. No data is sent to our servers — the sharing is handled entirely by your device's operating system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
